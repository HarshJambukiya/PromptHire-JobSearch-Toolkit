💻Domain-specific tips — Web Dev, DevOps, IT, ML
▼
Web Development: Build 2–3 live projects (deployed on Vercel/Netlify, not just GitHub). React + Node.js + MongoDB (MERN) is the most in-demand stack for freshers. A portfolio website is mandatory.

DevOps / Cloud: Set up a personal AWS/GCP free-tier account and document what you build. A working CI/CD pipeline on GitHub Actions + Docker deployment is worth more than any certificate. AWS Solutions Architect Associate cert is the best ROI cert to get hired faster.

AI / ML: A working RAG pipeline, live Streamlit app, or documented Kaggle notebook beats generic resumes. LangChain + vector DB experience is rare at fresher level and gets immediate attention.

IT Support / QA: ISTQB Foundation certification is widely recognised and takes ~1 month to prepare. For IT support, CompTIA A+ or Google IT Support certificate (free on Coursera) is enough for a first job.

Cybersecurity: TryHackMe and HackTheBox profiles are treated as direct experience. CEH or CompTIA Security+ are valued certificates. Bug bounty participation (HackerOne, Bugcrowd) is a strong differentiator.
🚫Top mistakes freshers make (and how to fix them)
▼
Mistake 1: Mass applying without tailoring. Sending one resume to 100 jobs gets 0 calls. Tailor the skills section to each job description's exact keywords — ATS filters out 75% of generic resumes before a human sees them.

Mistake 2: Only using one platform. Using 5+ platforms gives 3× more recruiter views. Don't rely only on LinkedIn or Naukri — use Cutshort for tech, Hirist for premium, Internshala for internships, and Unstop for challenges.

Mistake 3: No live projects or GitHub. 80% of employers now prioritise skills over degrees. A live deployed project with a clean README is worth more than listing "Python" on your resume for the 10th time.

Mistake 4: Ignoring the hidden job market. 70% of jobs are never publicly posted. Cold emailing a founder or team lead can create an opening that didn't exist. Direct outreach on LinkedIn has 2× higher response than job applications.

Mistake 5: No follow-up. 80% of candidates never follow up. One polite email 5–7 days after applying or messaging doubles your response rate significantly.

🤖How to use AI tools for job search — step by step
▼
1. Generate your prompt above. Be specific about your domain, skills, and what makes you unique.

2. Job Search prompt → open Claude.ai (enable Web Search) or Perplexity. Paste the prompt. Both search live job boards and return structured results with links.

3. Cold Email / LinkedIn DM → paste into Claude or ChatGPT. Always personalise by replacing [Company Name] with real research about that specific company's product or news.

4. Resume Review → paste your actual resume text into Claude after the prompt. Ask: "Here is my resume: [paste]. Now apply the review above." Get ATS score + rewrite suggestions instantly.

5. Interview Prep → tell Claude: "Act as a mock interviewer. Ask me one question at a time and give feedback after each answer." Do 2–3 full sessions before any real interview.
